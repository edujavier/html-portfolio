The Best Movies According to Eduardo Del Pozo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Ironman

Tony Stark, a wealthy and brilliant inventor, is captured by terrorists and forced to build a weapon.
Instead, he creates a powerful suit of armor to escape. Upon returning home, he improves the suit and becomes Iron Man.
He decides to stop making weapons and fight against those who use them for evil.
Tony eventually battles his former business partner, Obadiah Stane, who has created a similar suit.
Tony wins and reveals his identity as Iron Man to the public.
This film marks the beginning of Tony's journey as a superhero in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Spider-Man

Peter Parker is a teenager who goes to high school. He is also Spider-Man, a superhero.
He lives with his Aunt May. Peter wants to prove that he is a good hero. He looks up to Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man.
Peter discovers that a man named Adrian Toomes is selling dangerous weapons. Toomes is the Vulture.
He uses technology from battles involving the Avengers. Peter decides to stop him.
Peter struggles with being both a student and a superhero. He makes mistakes and learns from them.
In the end, Peter defeats the Vulture by himself. He chooses to stay a "friendly neighborhood Spider-Man" instead of joining the Avengers.
This movie shows Peter growing up and becoming a responsible hero.

The Transporter

Frank Martin is a professional transporter who follows strict rules for his job.
One day, he breaks one of his rules and discovers that his latest package is a woman named Lai.
This leads him into a dangerous human trafficking ring. Frank uses his exceptional skills to fight off enemies and protect Lai.
Together, they expose the traffickers and bring them to justice.
It's a high-octane action film with thrilling chase scenes and intense combat.
